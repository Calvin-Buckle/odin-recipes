Fancy Hot Chocolate

Description:

This is a thick and sweet cup of hot chocolate,

That i'm willing to bet you have never tried before.

Follow this easy recipe and you will have,

a new favorite way of making Hot Chocolate!

Ingredients:
  • Milk: 1,1/2 cup
  • Dark chocolate: 120 Grams
  • Sugar: 2 tbs

Steps:

  1. Chop up the Chocolate
  2. Add the Chocolate into a pot over medium heat
  3. Add a splash of milk to the pot
  4. Add the sugar
  5. Stir until melted
  6. Slowly start pouring in the milk, make sure to whisk properly while pouring the milk
  7. Continue to heat and stir until the HotChocolate is thickened
  8. Serve the hot chocolate!
